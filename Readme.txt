1. Filtering of products by brand. Users are able to filter products by brand through the checkboxes on the left. The brands checked will only be shown on the products page.

2. Cart. Users are able to add products to the cart and get a summary of the products in cart. Total price and products information will be shown on the cart summary page.

3. Search product name from any page. User will be able to search products on any page of the web application. After clicking search, the products page will be updated to only show the products that matches the search term. Filters can also be used together with this feature to get more specific results.
